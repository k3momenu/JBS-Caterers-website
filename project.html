About Us

JBS caterers is a food catering business that is co-owned by three beautiful women. These three women are not just co-owners of this business but they are working mothers and wives. The start of this business was sparked by the intrest in food and the passion they had for cooking. Seeing people enjoy themselves over the food that we made, warms our hearts and drives us to work even harder. We only cook mordern-traditional food because we see the gap in the food catering business for traditional food with a twist and we want to keep traditional cooking methods alive and give the world a piece of our heritage.
JBS will always be about Traditional food with a mordern twist. It's time to blow your taste buds and explode your senses!
You can count on us!
